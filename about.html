UW Enactus

Since re-establishing the club, we've taken on 3 projects, PickWaste, FEM in STEM and the Young Funds Program. Each project aims to address a different social issue in our community. Enactus Waterloo has continuously grown and expanded further into the community each year. We strive to help students develop new skills and empower them to become future leaders, all while making a positive impact in the community.

Competitions


All of the efforts of all Enactus chapters cultivates every year in Regional and National competitions. These events allow passionate and like-minded students to gather and showcase their impact on stage.
